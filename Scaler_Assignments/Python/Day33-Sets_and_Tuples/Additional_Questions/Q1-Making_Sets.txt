Q1. Making Sets

Which of the options defines a set s consisting of strings 'Scaler', 'Academy', 'is', and 'Awesome'?

s = set(['Scaler', 'Academy', 'is', 'Awesome'])
s = set('Scaler', 'Academy', 'is', 'Awesome')
s = set('Scaler Academy is Awesome')
s ={'Scaler', 'Academy', 'is', 'Awesome'}

solution:

s = set(['Scaler', 'Academy', 'is', 'Awesome'])
s ={'Scaler', 'Academy', 'is', 'Awesome'}
