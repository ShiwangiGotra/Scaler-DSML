Q2. Simple Maths

Problem Description:

Perform the following operations on 6 and 3 and print their respective outputs in different lines.

1. Addition
2. Subtraction
3. Multiplication
4. Division


Output Format

Print the output of 6+3, 6-3, 6*3, 6/3 in separate lines.


Example Output

9
3
18
2.0


Example Explanation

6 + 3 = 9
6 - 3 = 3
6 * 3 = 18
6 / 3 = 2.0
