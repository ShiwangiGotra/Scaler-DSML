Q4. Removing item

Consider the dictionary: prices = {'apple': 1.50, 'banana': 0.75, 'orange': 1.25}.

Which method(s) would you use to remove the banana key-value pair? Choose all that are correct.

a) prices.remove('banana')

b) del prices['banana']

c) prices.clear('banana')

d) prices.pop('banana')

Solution: b, d
